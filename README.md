CrashLens is a data-driven road accident analysis project designed to help government authorities, transport departments, and safety agencies understand accident patterns and casualty trends using interactive dashboards and business intelligence techniques.

The project focuses on analyzing road accident data from 2021 and 2022 to uncover insights related to accident severity, vehicle involvement, road conditions, and temporal trends, enabling data-backed decision-making for road safety improvements.
